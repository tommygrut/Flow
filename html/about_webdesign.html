Webdesign
The first year students attending computer engineering at Oslo Metropolitan University are each year given a final project in the subject “Webprosjekt” where they are supposed to find a problem and present a solution to this problem. Six students came together and started working on the assignment by brainstorming different problems and solutions until the idea Flow was born.

Commonly asked questions about Flow
What is Flow?
Flow is a subscription based service that gives the customer access to all the articles behind the paywalls of the newspaper companies that are under our service. We are looking to collaborate with more newspaper in the future. This includes both paid and free newspaper services.
Why does Flow exist?
Many people read news online, but some articles are locked behind the distributors paid services. For example, VG have the paid service VG+. People must also go to different news sites to find different information. Some sites may focus on sport while others focus on economy. Flow exists to gather all news articles paid and free, and to give the people the option to sort by the news they want to see across multiple news websites.
Will Flow improve their services in the future?
Yes! In the future Flow will strive towards implementing better translations of foreign news to the users preferred language to expand the number of articles they can read. Flow will also expand the preference and sort sections to give the user a more tailored experience. For example, sort by: most viewed – today, -week, -year. And adding show: cute, scientific, terror, pet articles.
What separates Flow from other similar sites?
Flows goal is to gather sizeable news sites from around the world in one place. In contrast other sites focus on helping publishing smaller local newspapers in their respective places.
